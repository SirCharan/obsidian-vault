### DeFi Strategy Discussion

- Exploring leveraged stablecoin yield farming

- Private credit tokens yielding 11-12% consistently
- $150k minimum deposit, 2-3 day redemption periods
- Proposed mechanism:

1. Use ETH as collateral
2. Borrow USDC against ETH
3. Invest USDC in yield-bearing stablecoin (MF1)
4. Execute in one-click interface

### Strategy Analysis & Risks

- Net yield calculation

- ETH exposure + yield differential (yield minus borrow rate)
- Estimated 1-2% net yield after costs
- Similar payoff to liquid staking derivatives (LSD) but with added complexity
- Key risks identified:

- Liquidation risk if ETH price drops
- 10-15% liquidation penalty could wipe out 3+ months of gains
- USDC borrow rates fluctuating (currently 4-5% base)
- Redemption challenges:

- Monthly redemption queues for yield-bearing tokens
- NAV discounts during stressed conditions
- Need instant redemption for position unwinding

### Market Maker Opportunity Assessment

- Uniswap V3/V4 concentrated liquidity positions

- ETH-USDC pair on Hyperliquid
- 10% base yield opportunity
- Requires delta/gamma hedging maintenance
- Market structure analysis:

- Most DeFi yield currently from memecoin speculation
- 90% of TVL concentrated in single protocol on Monad
- Capital double-counted across platforms

### Monad Token Economics

- Token unlock schedule concerns:

- 65% of tokens unlocking starting November
- 10% current circulation, 2% annual inflation
- Linear vesting of remaining supply
- Risk assessment: potential price pressure from unlock events

### Strategic Recommendation

- Limited alpha in proposed ETH collateral strategy

- 1-2% net yield insufficient for complexity and risks
- Better alternatives exist with simpler execution
- Focus areas for experimentation:

- Market making opportunities if properly hedged
- Euler-aligned fund products for distribution
- Tokenized vault structures for broader access
