### Crypto Launchpad Strategy

- Permissionless ERC20 token launchpad launching directly to Uniswap V4

- Skipping bonding curves (unlike pump.fun)
- Using V4 for additional features over V3
- Creator revenue model: 50% of Uniswap transaction fees shared

- 0.5% fee on trades (less than 20% of pump.fun fees)
- All swap fees earned in both tokens of trading pair
- Revenue collection mechanism: charging swap fees only in USDC

- Protocol earns stable revenue while tokens fluctuate
- Creators earn in USDC regardless of token performance

### Perpetual Futures Integration

- No-loss, liquidation-free perps available from day zero

- Zero technical restrictions across all tokens
- Novel funding fee structure creates protocol revenue

- No counterparty needed - all funding fees become protocol revenue
- Can charge 0-100% funding fees to incentivize trade closure
- Revenue split with creators
- Breakthrough concept: 0% fees possible while still generating revenue

- Traders forced to close positions through funding mechanism
- “Broken the market” with this launchpad + perps combination

### Prediction Markets & Floor Price Innovation

- Price prediction markets for meme coins from launch

- 10x payoff structures (pay $0.10-0.20 for $1 payout)
- Premium payments become protocol revenue
- Revolutionary floor price mechanism using accumulated swap fees

- Formula: Total revenue ÷ Free float tokens = Floor price
- Example: $10 swap fees ÷ 10 tokens = $1 floor price
- Single-sided USDC liquidity absorbs all selling pressure
- HyperLiquid comparison: $10B revenue ÷ 350M tokens = $30 floor price

- Current price $40, so maximum loss only $10 vs full $40 risk
