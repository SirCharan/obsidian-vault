### Hackathon Project Overview

- Building meme coin launchpad for hackathon with 25 lakh prize potential
- Already have working product (perps) - just need design refinement
- Current platform includes:

- Meme coin discovery page with categories (trending, fast growing, etc.)
- Trading page with market data, buy/sell functionality
- Launch flow (3-step form for coin creation)
- Platform captures “attention markets” - people betting on meme virality
- Creators earn from trading fees beyond price appreciation

### Competitive Analysis & Design Direction

- Reviewed major platforms:

- Pump.fun: Most successful ($8B valuation, $500M raised) but basic design
- Clanker/Noise: Minimalist approach, backed by Coinbase/Balaji
- Launch: Focus on creator earnings/buybacks
- Hyperliquid-based platforms: Clean, professional designs
- Design preferences emerging:

- Full-screen layouts over centered components with margins
- Medium-sized images (between small/large competitors)
- Base blockchain theme (blue colors, glitch effects)
- Need mascot consideration for branding
