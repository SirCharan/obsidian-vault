# Demo Script

> [!tip] 7-step walkthrough for showing Stocky AI to someone

## Step 1: The Hook (10s)
Open llm.stockyai.xyz. Show the terminal-style boot animation. Say: "This is Stocky AI -- like Bloomberg Terminal meets ChatGPT for Indian markets."

## Step 2: Quick Analysis (30s)
Type: **"analyse RELIANCE"**
- Show the AnalysisCard with Stocky Score 0-20
- Point out the 4-factor breakdown (Fundamental/Technical/Sentiment/Macro)
- Highlight the scenario table (Bull/Base/Bear with targets)
- Show the trade setup (Entry/SL/Target/R:R)

## Step 3: Market Overview (20s)
Click the **Market Overview** button.
- Show indices, breadth, gainers/losers
- Point out the AI market regime call (RISK-ON/OFF/MIXED)
- Highlight the risk-reward of the day

## Step 4: Multi-Agent Debate (60s)
Type: **"council research on HDFC Bank"**
- Watch the CouncilProgressCard render each agent in real-time
- Point out the 6 agents: Technical, Fundamental, Market Pulse, Risk, Macro, CSO
- Explain: "Heavy agents (TS, FA, CSO) use 70B for depth, light agents use 17B for speed"
- Show the final verdict with confidence score

## Step 5: Options Analytics (30s)
Click **Options** and type: **"NIFTY"**
- Show PCR analysis (contrarian indicator explanation)
- Point out Max Pain vs Spot distance
- Show the strategy recommendations table

## Step 6: News Intelligence (20s)
Click **News**.
- Show 41 RSS feeds aggregated with sentiment scoring
- Point out the AI impact matrix and contrarian thesis

## Step 7: The Architecture (30s)
Briefly mention: "Under the hood: Next.js 16 + React 19 frontend on Vercel, FastAPI backend on Railway, 4 LLM models across 6 API keys in round-robin. 31 handlers, 27 card components, 14 database tables. All built in 60 days."

## Closing
"Stocky AI gives you institutional-grade research through an AI chat interface. One message can trigger a 6-agent debate or execute a trade through your broker."
