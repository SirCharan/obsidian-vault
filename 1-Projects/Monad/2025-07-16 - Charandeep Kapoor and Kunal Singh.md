### Protocol Overview - TimeLog

- Building liquidation-free leverage trading platform on Monad
- Novel mechanism borrowing tick liquidity from Uniswap V3 LPs
- Offers up to 1000x leverage without liquidation risk
- Fully collateralized positions - traders pay premium for upside only
- Currently live on Tenderly fork of Base mainnet as demo
- ~6,000 lines of code, complex protocol requiring audit

### Product Mechanism

- Borrows tick liquidity from Uniswap V3, keeps it in protocol escrow
- LPs remain risk-neutral vs GMX model where trader PnL settled against pool
- Oracle-less, governance-free - all prices derived from Uniswap
- Example: Pay $6 to long 1 ETH at $3,100 for 1 hour

- If ETH rises $100+, trader captures full upside
- If ETH drops $1,000, trader loses only $6 premium
- Single-sided USDC deposits into vaults with professional vault managers
