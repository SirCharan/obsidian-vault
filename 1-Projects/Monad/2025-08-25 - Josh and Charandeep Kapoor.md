### Time Lock Protocol Overview

- Building liquidation-free leverage trading mechanism

- Borrows tick liquidity from Uniswap V3 vs traditional orderbook/peer-to-pool systems
- Creates fully collateralized, non-liquidable positions with payoffs resembling options
- Example: Pay 6 USDC premium for 1 hour ETH long at 782x leverage - capture all upside, max loss is premium
- Development status

- Live on Monad testnet (internal testing only)
- Deployed on Tenderly fork of Base mainnet for public demo
- Waiting on Monad liquidity provision before public launch
- 7,000+ lines of code, partial audits completed
- Product roadmap from same codebase

- Time Lock Trades (fixed duration leverage)
- Time Lock Perps (perpetual with funding rates)
- Time Lock Options (OTM tick borrowing)
- Time Lock Swap (custom Uniswap V3 fork)
