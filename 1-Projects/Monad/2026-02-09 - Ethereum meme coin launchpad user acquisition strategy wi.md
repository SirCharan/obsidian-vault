### Platform Economics & User Acquisition Challenge

- Core problem: need simultaneous onboarding of thousands of users

- Token creators need traders present
- Traders need market makers
- Market makers need volume
- Competitive advantage over Pump.fun:

- Pump.fun creators only profit from pump/dump of own tokens
- Platform shares perps and spot trading revenue with creators
- Chicken-and-egg problem requires mass user acquisition, not gradual onboarding
