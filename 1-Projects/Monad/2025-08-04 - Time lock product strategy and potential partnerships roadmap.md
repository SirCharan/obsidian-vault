### Key Insights from Monad Founder’s Dinner

- Product complexity presents scaling challenges

- Time lock mechanism too complex for first-time explanations
- Built high-IQ technical product without sufficient market credibility
- Unlike funded protocols (Morpho, Eigen Layer), lack pedigree to justify complexity
- Connected with IIT seniors (2014-18 batches) building on Monad

- Rag Rumble project team offered partnership support
- Consumer-focused approach vs. current technical differentiation

### Strategic Product Pivot Options

- Casino product deployment on Monad instead of options product

- Easier user understanding and explanation
- Leverages trading background for yield strategies
- Partnership with Nerd.Fun for meme coin shorting

- Enable 1x leverage shorting of meme coins (unique offering)
- Nerd.Fun handles front-end and user acquisition
- Time lock provides contract infrastructure only

### Yield Strategy Opportunities

- High-net-worth individual (Rahul) interested in yield strategies

- Looking to invest $100K initially, scaling to $1-3M
- Targeting 15% yield through trading strategies
- LP vault revenue streams:

- Uniswap V3 swap fees
- Trader premiums
- Managed payoff optimization

### Product Development Roadmap

- Current time frames (20min, 1hr, 3hr, 4hr) have lower success probability
- Casino-style shorter timeframes (1min, 2min, 5min):

- Mobile-first design required
- Fixed USDC pricing instead of premium/IV complexity
- Example: $1 for 5min ETH trade, $10 for BTC
- Future expansion possibilities:

- Perps (hourly contract rollovers)
- Options (multiple strike prices)
- Futures
- Own L2 launch

### Social Media Strategy Improvements

- Number presentation guidelines:

- Use full numbers with commas, not “k” abbreviations
- Focus on relatable profit examples ($10 → $500)
- Include actual trade scenarios with real market data
- Content diversification:

- Add LP vault benefits alongside trader liquidation content
- Create “Hyperliquid vs Time Lock” comparison threads
- Include live market examples with charts

### Partnership and Funding Strategy

- Alternative partnerships: other meme coin launchpads on different chains
- Funding approach: angels first, then larger VCs
- Market validation: need deployed product before major fundraising
