- Backed by VanEck (Nick VanEck as CEO, son of Jan VanEck)
- Infrastructure: State Street Bank custody, PwC auditing, VanEck asset management
- Revenue model: 4% yield from T-bills/reverse repo, sharing 3.2-3.5% with partners
- Can share up to 80-90% revenue with Monad ecosystem (vs USDC 0%)

### TimeLog Product Integration Potential

- TimeLog borrows liquidity from Uniswap V3 pools (not P2P derivatives)
- Mechanism: Borrow ETH at spot, profit from price appreciation, repay with swap proceeds
- Can build on any deep/liquid AMM pools (WMON/AUSD, WMON/USDC, etc)
- Two vault types offered:

- Standard Uniswap V3 payoff + trader premium yields (paid in stablecoin)
- Delta-neutral stable vaults (hedged via perp shorts)
