### TimeLock Product Updates

- Live TimeLock Perps product with onboarded partner

- Permissionless markets for any memecoin/USDC pair deployments
- Built on Uniswap v3/v4 with custom hooks enabling:

- Zero fees for NFT holders/early adopters
- Automated weekly buybacks from swap fees
- All features offered free to attract projects from other deployments
- Liquidation-free trading model

- No margin deposits required - pay fixed funding directly from wallet
- Positions fully collateralized by design
- Trades execute without wallet confirmations for smooth UX
- Built on perpetual options (disguised as perps) - traders only pay premium, no losses on wrong side
- Focus shifted from ETH/large tokens to memecoins/altcoins

- Easier to onboard projects directly vs competing for large trade liquidity
- Revenue sharing model similar to Hyperliquid
- One week onboarding time, reducing to self-service in 2-3 weeks

### Strategic Direction & Fundraising

- Staying with Monad ecosystem for now

- Direct connections to 10+ projects ready to onboard
- Uncertainty around Uniswap incentives post-Monad Momentum
- Current valuation expectations: $50-100M (vs offered $10-20M)

- Self-funded by founder, no external capital raised yet
- Waiting for product traction before fundraising
- Believes liquidation-free product will drive automated marketing once live
- Product roadmap includes custom duration options

- Any expiry down to block-level precision (100.4 seconds on Monad)
- Targeting institutional users and hedge funds for hedging locked positions
- Currently killing features to simplify retail products

### LP Program Discussion

- Cannot guarantee fixed 30% APY in cash as previously discussed

- Advised against fixed dollar commitments due to black swan risk
- Market-determined APY from swap fees + short-term funding rates + trader premiums
- Potential for >30% during high usage periods
- Alternative structure proposed:

- Base yield from protocol mechanics
- Points/future token allocation to fill gaps vs 30% hurdle rate
- Larger allocation consideration for substantial LP commitments
- Oscar confirmed SCB’s 30% hurdle rate requirement

- Need strategic partnership component beyond pure yield
- Will evaluate based on Monad vs Uniswap decision and program updates
