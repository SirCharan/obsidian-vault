### Product Overview & Market Strategy

- TimeKeeper Timelock building unified derivatives platform on top of Uniswap V3/V4 liquidity

- Single codebase enables multiple products: options, perps, swaps, launchpad
- Currently live on Monad testnet with 2-2.5M volume, 100+ traders
- Partnered with 2 meme coins on Monad
- Product suite includes:

- Timelock Options - custom strike/expiry European options on any Uniswap-supported asset
- Timelock Trade - ATM options with 1hr-7day duration (currently live)
- Timelock Perps - perpetual options replacing traditional perps (no liquidations)
- Timelock Swap - Uniswap V3/V4 with vault management
- Launchpad - meme coin launches with built-in derivatives from day zero

### Derek’s Strategic Feedback

- Concern about competing across 3 major DeFi categories simultaneously

- Recommended focusing on 1-2 products with strongest conviction
- Perps identified as largest market with clear PMF due to liquidation problems
- Market opportunity validation

- $1B+ perps liquidated recently during market volatility
- Options inevitable as assets mature institutionally
- Historical pattern: options achieve 5-10x futures volume once established

### Fundraising Challenges & Advice

- Current funding situation: Down to last $20-25k of initial $100k bootstrap
- Two main VC categories creating pitch difficulties:

- Non-DeFi VCs don’t understand options/derivatives
- DeFi-savvy VCs don’t grasp Uniswap short straddle exploitation
- Derek’s fundraising recommendations:

- Approach with more leverage (heat on round or live product traction)
- Consistently zoom out to bigger picture opportunity
- Anchor in familiar concepts (e.g., “10x better perps experience, no liquidations”)
- Don’t assume derivatives knowledge - provide simple framings
- Consider seed rounds need “dumber” pitches than later rounds
