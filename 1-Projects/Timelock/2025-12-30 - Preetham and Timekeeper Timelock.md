### Timelock Product Overview

- Multi-DeFi platform combining perps, options, prediction markets via Uniswap v3
- Perps and options currently live; prediction markets in development
- Options targeting institutions via APIs/RFQ models, not retail front-end
- Permissionless market creation for any token size
- Oracle-less, no liquidations, no market makers, no ADLs

### Current Status & Immediate Plans

- Monad testnet reset, moving to mainnet launch
- Self-funding 30-40k USDC for initial Uniswap pool demonstration
- Launch timeline: 1-2 weeks (vs Rise’s February timeline)
- Planning fundraising round starting January
- One audit complete, seeking 3-4 additional tier-one audits

### Revenue Model & Value Proposition

- Multiple revenue streams without Oracle/liquidation infrastructure costs:

1. Trading fees from users
2. Cut of funding fees
3. Percentage of LP earnings
- Native stablecoin generating 15-20% APY
- Users paying 1% daily fees willingly for liquidation-free perps
- No incentives needed due to high organic demand

### Partnership Discussion with Rise

- Timelock seeks marketing partnership, not financial incentives
- Rise interested due to non-competing nature and broader token support
- Preetham to discuss with Apoorv and Sam this week
- Rise team currently has limited bandwidth
- Potential revenue sharing: Rise wants $0.20 return per $1 incentive provided

### Permissionless Launchpad Vision

- Building complete token lifecycle: launch → swap → perps/prediction markets
- Allows even known scammers to launch (anti-cabal approach)
- Enables perps trading during launch period (day zero)
- 25% of raised funds locked for one year in liquidity pool
- Targeting founders building on Rise ecosystem for direct partnerships
