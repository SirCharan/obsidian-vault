### TimeKeeper Product Overview

- Liquidation-free perpetuals exchange using everlasting options
- Simple UX: click long/short, enter position, pay premium
- Up to 2400x leverage with no liquidation risk
- Call option payoff structure - only upside after premium paid
- Current example: $300k TVL generating $2.5M trading volume in 7 days
- Permissionless markets for any Uniswap-deployed token

### Technical Integration Approach

- TimeKeeper Swap: Uniswap V3/V4 fork

- Zero-fee swaps for NFT holders
- Sell tax management
- Automatic buybacks from fees
- Projects deploy any token pair to TimeKeeper Swap
- Perpetuals enabled from day zero for any liquidity size
- Minimum recommended: $20-50k liquidity for meaningful trading
