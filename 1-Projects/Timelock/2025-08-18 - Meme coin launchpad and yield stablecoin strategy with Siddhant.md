- Market opportunity

- Solana alone: $5-10B

### Technical Implementation

- Bonding curve graduation process

- After 100k USDC deposited, bonding curve closes
- Time Lock deploys liquidity instead of launchpad
- Creates full-range UniV3 pool with 100k USDC + 1M tokens
- Time Lock owns entire liquidity position
- Engineering advantages

- Only need contract development
- Open source approach for developer adoption
- No rebalancing required (full range positions)
- Cross-chain compatibility planned
