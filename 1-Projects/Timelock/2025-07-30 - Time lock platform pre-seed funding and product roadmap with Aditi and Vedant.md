### TimeLog DeFi Protocol Overview

- Building TimeLog - a DeFi protocol with unique trading mechanism

- Peer-to-pool trading model (similar to casino house vs gamblers)
- Oracle-less, liquidation-free trading mechanism
- Offers 1000x leverage on any ERC-20 token without liquidation
- Key product differentiators:

- Borrows liquidity from Uniswap V3 LPs
- No active market makers required (eliminates conflict of interest)
- Enables trading for any token with a Uniswap V3 pool
- Focus on shorter timeframe trades (1 min, 5 min, etc.)
- Technical implementation

- Forked contracts from Dopex for trading side
- Forked Orange Finance for LP/vault side
- Currently 80% complete, targeting Monad testnet deployment
