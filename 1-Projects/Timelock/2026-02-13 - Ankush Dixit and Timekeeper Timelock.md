### Ankush’s Product - Microfactory

- Building validation platform for teams of 2-4 members
- Pre-building phase tool - validates ideas before development

- Different from Lovable (builds apps) or Product Hunt (post-launch voting)
- Team ideation → blind feedback → quick build → launch
- Nearly complete, launching in 1-2 days
- Plans to test on Hacker News and other platforms

### Charandeep’s Launchpad Concept

- “Build, launch, trade, bet on anything” platform
- Token-based voting system replacing traditional voting

- People invest capital instead of simple votes
- Captures strength of belief through financial commitment
- Two-phase launch process:

1. 24-hour IPO phase - creator dilutes 10-20% equity, all proceeds go to creator
2. Secondary market trading - only transaction fees flow to creator
- Transaction fees provide perpetual revenue stream for creators

- Example: Zomato got money once at IPO, but ongoing trading activity could benefit creators

### Ankush’s Concerns About Crypto Barrier

- Main reservation: audience limited to crypto-aware users
- Friction of crypto payments excludes mainstream users
- Works well for crypto-related projects, less suitable for general products
- Acknowledges concept is strong but payment method creates adoption barrier

### Partnership Discussion

- Charandeep seeking partnerships with creator platform builders
- Microfactory could bring creators to launch on Charandeep’s platform
- Competitor reference: OpenClaw hackathon with similar token mechanics

- Less sophisticated than Charandeep’s model (no IPO, no fee sharing)

### Next Steps

- Ankush to launch Microfactory within 1-2 days
- Will assess traffic and creator adoption over 1-2 weeks
- If successful traction, will explore partnership to promote Charandeep’s platform to creators
- Ankush to provide updates via WhatsApp
