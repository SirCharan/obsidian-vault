### Product Strategy & Market Focus

- Core business model: signals as primary revenue driver

- People only pay for trading signals, not other features
- Target 1-2K per person subscription pricing
- Three product options identified:

- Signals + live updates/notifications system
- Watcher Guru-style news aggregation
- Screener-style stock analysis tools
- GTM1 selected: signals + live updates with multi-channel notifications

- App notifications, WhatsApp, Telegram, Email
- Mobile-first approach preferred
- Free initially, then 1-2K monthly subscription

### Marketplace Vision (GTM2)

- Build signals marketplace instead of just personal curation

- Onboard existing Telegram/YouTube signal providers
- Let creators bring their audiences to platform
- Solve payment management pain points for signal providers
- Revenue model: 10-12% commission on creator earnings

- Example: Akanksha makes 6cr monthly charging 6K to 10K people
- Platform handles payments, subscriptions, access control automatically
- Competitive advantage: easier setup, better UX than Telegram groups

- No manual payment verification needed
- Unified platform access vs scattered channels

### Technical Implementation & Next Steps

- VPN solution needed for consistent Zerodha access

- Same IP across devices to prevent logouts
- Auto-retry login system every 5 minutes if disconnected
- Team structure post-funding:

- 100K seed funding target identified
- Full-time transition required post-funding for team
- Aditya and others get salary, Charandeep takes no salary initially
- MVP development priorities:

- Railway deployment over AWS for simplicity
- Mobile-first UI similar to Spotlight design
- Testing with LinkedIn network once ready
