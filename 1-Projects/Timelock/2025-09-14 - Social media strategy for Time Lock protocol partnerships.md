### Product Overview Explanation

- Building non-liquidable leverage trading ecosystem

- Industry-first approach similar to Panoptic protocol
- Allows $1k users to execute $1M trades with 1000x leverage
- No liquidation risk through financial engineering
- Three core products planned:

- Time Lock Trade - leverage without liquidations
- Time Lock Perps - perpetuals alternative
- Time Lock Options - options trading
- Additional products:

- UniV3-like AMM with automated vaults (eliminates range selection/rebalancing)
- Time Lock Meme - meme coin launchpad with shorting capability

### Current Market Problems Being Solved

- Traditional exchanges (Hyperliquid, Lighter) dominate but have liquidation risks
- Pump.fun creates zero-cost scam tokens with no shorting ability
- Information flow limited on smaller tokens due to lack of derivatives
- UniSwap range management complexity for users

### Go-to-Market Strategy

- Starting on Monad testnet with partnerships across 3 DeFi protocols
- B2B service model initially - partners handle users/marketing, TimeLog provides contracts
- Revenue sharing partnerships from day zero
- Transition to direct consumer products after proving PMF
- Multi-chain expansion planned (Arbitrum, Base, Hyperliquid)
