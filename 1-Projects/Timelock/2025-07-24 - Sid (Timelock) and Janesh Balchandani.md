### TimeLock Product Overview

- Liquidation-free leverage trading offering up to 1000x leverage on major assets (BTC) and meme coins
- Fixed upfront premium model instead of traditional liquidation risk

- Example: Long ETH for 1 hour costs only 6 USDC, capture full upside regardless of downside movement
- Borrows liquidity from P3 LPs to enable fully collateralized positions
- Oracle-less system using Uniswap AMM liquidity as source of truth
- 7-8 months development, currently live on Tenderly fork of Base mainnet
- 6,000 lines of novel code (not forked), fully bootstrapped with $1.5-2k monthly burn

### Feros Chain Capabilities & Features

- Performance: 2 gigas per second (2-2.5x faster than Monad’s 1 giga)
- Block speeds under 2 milliseconds, 20-30k TPS on devnet/testnet, targeting 50k TPS on mainnet
- Two major DEXs: Zenith (Uniswap v2 fork) and FerroSwap (blend of Uniswap v2/v3)
- Oracle solutions: Chainlink on mainnet, Supra Oracle on testnet (pull and push)
- Infrastructure support: Goldsky subgraphs, Mirror for faster data pipelines

### Experimental Product Ideas

- Casino-style ultra-short duration trades (1-5 minutes vs current 1 hour-3 day)

- $10-12 per trade, rapid up/down button clicking interface
- Protocol bribing mechanism for targeted trades

- Competing protocols can incentivize shorts against competitors
- Ecosystem partners can drive longs on specific tokens
- Real spot/leverage trading (not derivatives) actually moves markets
