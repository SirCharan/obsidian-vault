### Time Lock Protocol Overview

- Planning for Time Lock, a DeFi protocol with novel time-lock mechanism

- No liquidation feature - positions offloaded instead of liquidated
- 1000x leverage on ERC-20 tokens without liquidation
- Fixed upfront premium, no variable borrower rates or funding rates
- For traders:

- Easy UX compared to competitors with complicated interfaces
- Linear payoff structure versus nonlinear outcomes from competitors
- For LPs (Liquidity Providers):

- One-click deposit into managed vaults
- Vaults actively managed by strategy creators
- UniV3 vaults potentially offering 22.5% APY at 50% utilization
- UniV3 vault hedge to create stablecoin vault
