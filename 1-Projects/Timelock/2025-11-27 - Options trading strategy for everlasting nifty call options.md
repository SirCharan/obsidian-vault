### Everlasting Futures Concept

- Continuous future contract without expiry

- Eliminates need for manual rollover each month
- Fixed cost: 150 rupees monthly or 5 rupees daily
- Automatic position maintenance for perpetual exposure
- Mathematical equivalence to buying recurring futures

- Same P&L as spot Nifty price movements
- Removes operational complexity of managing expirations

### Everlasting Options Strategy

- Call options extended to continuous contracts

- Weekly premium payment vs lump sum at purchase
- ATM call options: 26,000 strike example discussed
- Automatic rollover system prevents expiration losses
- Risk profile comparison

- Maximum loss limited to premium paid
- Unlimited upside potential maintained
- Premium typically 0.5% of position size (negligible)

### Cost Structure Analysis

- Weekly vs monthly option premiums

- Weekly: 120 rupees example
- Monthly rollover creates 30 rupee profit opportunity
- Beast token integration for automated payments
- Position sizing implications

- 2x-3x leverage possible without significant token movement
- Percentage gains amplified relative to underlying moves
- Entry price becomes maximum loss threshold

### Options Pricing Mechanics

- Expiry-based cost variations

- ATM options: 25 rupees at expiry
- 6-day options: roughly 6 rupees premium
- Deep ITM options become more accessible
- Market timing flexibility improved with continuous structure
