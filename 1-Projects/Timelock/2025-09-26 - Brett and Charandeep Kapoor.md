### TimeLog Protocol Overview

- Building auxiliary perp DEX product to complement (not replace) existing exchanges like Hyperliquid, Aster
- Solves key problems current DEXs can’t address:

- $400M daily liquidations ($250k/minute) create unfair price wicks
- Heavy oracle/market maker reliance enables manipulation
- Retail traders consistently lose to market makers
- Limited asset availability beyond top 3-4 tokens
- Offers high leverage trading without liquidations

- Pay small premium (~$1) for full upside exposure on $400 ETH position
- No downside beyond premium paid
- Positions stay open for full duration regardless of price movement
- Similar to cash-settled European at-the-money options but without oracles/market makers

### Product Development & Roadmap

- Currently live on Monad/Base testnets with working demo
- Mechanism differs from Panoptic:

- Linear payoffs vs Panoptic’s complex nonlinear structure
- Simple UX vs Panoptic’s 10+ click process
- Suitable for hedging existing positions
- Planned product expansion:

- TimeLog Trade (current) - leveraged positions without liquidations
- Perps trading
- Custom strike/expiry options with OTM capabilities
- MON/USDC markets launching day zero of Monad mainnet
