### Product Portfolio & Strategy

- Three main products targeting different audiences:

- TimeLocal Options - B2B for market makers (no UI, RFQ-based)
- TimeLocal Perps - retail-focused
- TimeLocal Trade + stablecoin product
- Key differentiators from failed options protocols:

- AMM-based vs order books (no counterparty dependency)
- Oracle-less architecture
- No liquidations
- Permissionless market creation
- Monad ecosystem partnerships with 3-4 meme coin/token launchpads
- Revenue sharing model similar to Hyperliquid builder codes
