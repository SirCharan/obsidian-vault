### TimeLock Platform Overview

- Full-stack DeFi platform: token launchpad + swap + perps + options + prediction markets
- Currently live: launchpad, swap, perps functionality
- B2B model: no direct user acquisition/marketing - partners deploy infrastructure for their own tokens
- Live example: Meme Steroid (meme coin on Monad)

- Deployed PEROID/USDC swap pool using TimeLock infrastructure
- Currently has live perps on their own token

### Market Opportunity & Strategy

- Problem: 99% of tokens never get perps listings on major exchanges (Hyperliquid, Binance, etc.)
- Solution: Permissionless perps markets for any token
- Current focus: meme coins and altcoins
- Future expansion: real world assets (gold, Apple stock) when on-chain

### Multi-Chain Expansion Plan
