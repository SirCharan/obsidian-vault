### Partnership Overview

- Charandeep explained TimeLock Protocol: novel mechanism for trading leverage by borrowing idle tick liquidity from UniV3
- Enables trading any token with UniV3 pool without requiring liquidity, market makers, or oracles
- Key differentiator: ability to short meme coins (first time possible in DeFi)

### Proposed Collaboration Model

- Partnership approach rather than competing platform
- Dagavrikov’s team keeps existing platform and community
- TimeLog provides additional frontend for long/short functionality

- Revenue split on funding rates from perps
- No hijacking of existing token business
- Optional pro trader switcher UI with long/short buttons

### Technical Implementation

- 1x leverage long/short perps (no extensive leverage for meme coins)
- Fully collateralized trades with no liquidation risk
- Trader deposits $100 margin to short $100 of token

- All profit from price drops belongs to trader
- No loss if price goes up (only funding fee)
- Funding cost: ~$5/day (~$0.20-0.22/hour) for $100 position
- Requires smart contract deposits (current wallet connect insufficient)

- Dagavrikov’s platform not live yet (2 weeks to 1 month launch timeline)

- TimeLog integration planned for post-launch iteration
- TimeLog has live beta platform (invite-only access for testing)
