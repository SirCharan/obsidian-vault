### Product Overview: Time Lock Trade

- Permissionless perps platform for any ERC-20 token
- Enables trading without liquidations using novel mechanism
- Projects deposit tokens into Uniswap V3/V4 AMM
- Even 50k TVL enables $400 trade size (0.8-1% slippage)
- $1M TVL supports $10k trades without slippage
- Solves liquidity bootstrapping problem for smaller projects

### Value Proposition for Projects

- Revenue sharing model

- Projects get 50% of trading fees + funding fees
- No charge for Time Lock Swap features (Uniswap V4 hooks)
- Custom features via hooks

- Automated buybacks
- Project-specific functionality
- White-label deployment on project domains
- Co-branding with minimal Time Lock presence
