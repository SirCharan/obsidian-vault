### Trading Fees Abuse Incident

- High-frequency trader exploited zero-fee structure on their platform
- Made 20,000+ trades, 90% profitable through arbitrage between their platform and Binance
- Generated $20k profit using fast trading with very low slippage
- Platform stopped it by increasing fees, trader left
- Uses older Pyth oracle (not laser) while Binance updates faster, creating arbitrage opportunity

### Crypto Product Landscape Analysis

- Only 4-5 core DeFi products exist: spot, perps, lending, yield farming
- Innovation cycle slowing significantly

- Uniswap v2 to v3: 1 year gap
- v3 to v4: 4 years gap
- License periods extending (v3: 3 years, v4: 5 years)
- Building real products now costs millions, not just good ideas
- Most successful products need substantial funding or perfect timing

### Market Dynamics Discussion

- Crypto dominated by MEV/sandwich attacks, similar across chains
- Solana winning due to better UX for memecoin trading, not technical superiority
- Hyperliquid’s success attributed to $100M token pump rather than just product quality
- Traditional incumbents (banks) entering crypto space with significant capital
- Retail preference for perps over options due to complexity and leverage limitations
