- Launched mainnet Alpha today for Rise

- Everything sits on one shared EVM state for composability benefits

### Current Ecosystem & Deployment Status

- Timekeeper not currently live - was on Monad testnet before genesis reset
- No new deployment made yet post-testnet reset
- Previously ran PUSDC perps for 2 weeks on testnet for meme coin project
- Looking for ecosystem to become central part of, not just another deployment

### Timekeeper’s Full Stack Vision

- Comprehensive platform: spot trading → perps → options → prediction markets → timelock launchpad
- All built on Uniswap v4 hooks
- Two vault types:

1. Uniswap v4 vault (trader takes full IL + premium)
2. Delta-neutral stable vault (partnered with SPS Digital for hedging)
- Target: 25-30% yields on delta-neutral positions
- Focus on longer-tail assets, meme coins, future RWAs/equities

### Rise Ecosystem Constraints

- Rise launching with Aerodrome fork (no hooks initially)
- Uniswap v4 not prioritized for day-one launch
- Third-party team building central limit order book for top assets
- AMM-to-orderbook graduation system for liquidity threshold
- Native AMM would be adversarial to Timekeeper’s Uniswap v4 approach

### Competitive Positioning Discussion

- Risex targeting top 20-50 tokens initially via orderbooks
- Timekeeper focusing on long-tail assets via AMM
- Potential coexistence: Risex for majors, Timekeeper for meme coins/RWAs
- Rise’s composability advantages: shared margin, yield strategies, lending integration
- Market making challenges for long-tail assets in orderbook model
