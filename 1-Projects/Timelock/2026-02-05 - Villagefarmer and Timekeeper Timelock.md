### Timelock Product Overview

- Building perps without liquidations using idle liquidity from Uniswap V4
- Platform capabilities:

- Perps without liquidations (live on Monart)
- Options trading
- Lending/borrowing markets
- Recently launched meme coin and built meme coin launchpad

- Single-sided Uniswap V4 liquidity provision
- Uniswap CCA launchpad (not yet public)

### Builder Codes Partnership Model

- Concept similar to Hyperliquid’s builder codes
- Allows projects to:

- Use Timelock contracts to seed their own pools
- Create custom meme/USDC pairs
- Enable spot and perp trading on their domain
- Earn share of trading fees
- For Launch partnership: Timelock would build entire integration

- Identical contracts to Timelock’s launchpad
- Extensive V4 hooks implementation

### Meme Coin Derivatives Thesis

- Current launchpads enable pump-and-dump schemes
- Derivatives unlock shorting capability for overvalued tokens
- Market mechanisms:

- Short overvalued tokens → price correction
- Long undervalued tokens with leverage
- Short squeezes for incorrectly shorted tokens
- Goal: Accelerate information flow into token prices

### Launch Partnership Benefits

- For Launch:

- Perps capability = major differentiator vs competitors
- “Undisputed best launchpad on Base”
- One less competitor (Timelock won’t launch competing product)
- For Timelock:

- Access to Launch’s distribution
- Base team recommended partnering vs building separate launchpad
- Technical fit: Launch already uses V4 with custom swap fees
