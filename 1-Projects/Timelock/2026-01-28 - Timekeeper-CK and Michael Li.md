### Product Overview & Current State

- Timekeeper trading platform built on Monad

- Perpetual futures without liquidations - retail-friendly approach
- Options product for market makers with custom strikes/expiries
- Both products require Mon/USDC liquidity pair to launch
- Max spending limit model protects retail traders

- Set protocol-wide spending cap (like contract approval limits)
- Opening fee (~$1) + hourly funding rate (0.1% both sides)
- Prevents wallet drain common on platforms like Hyperliquid
