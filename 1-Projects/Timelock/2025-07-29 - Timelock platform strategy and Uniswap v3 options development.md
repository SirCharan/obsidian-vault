### Platform & Product Development

- Demo platform deployed on Tenderly testnet, in talks to deploy on Monad
- Monad partnership opportunity for token launch integration and risk trading
- Fundraising target reduced to $600k focused on current product + Monad integration

- Audit + legal costs: $200k upfront
- Team growth + BD: $150k
- Partnered with EFI for yield strategy agents
- Timeline: 3 months (July-September) for audit completion, then LP onboarding
- Two vault types planned:

- Uni V3 vaults - earn swap fees + trading premiums
- Stable vaults - hedged positions creating stablecoin payoff (22.5% APY at 50% utilization vs 15% market rate)

### Core Value Proposition & Market Positioning

- Positioned as “liquidation-free leverage” rather than options (better market reception)
- Arbitrage opportunity: LPs earn 0.7-0.8 BSM, traders pay 0.9 BSM
- Key advantages over competitors:

- Can build options for any token with Uni V3 pool (meme coins, protocol tokens)
- Lower cost than order book exchanges
- Non-liquidation feature (premium: 0.5% per day)
- Target customers: Event-driven traders, altcoin traders seeking higher leverage (200x vs 25-40x on other platforms)

### Advisor Feedback & Strategic Concerns

- Strong skepticism about supply-side (LP) focused approach vs demand-side (trader) focus
- Criticism of 2022-style DeFi yield farming model
- Advised focus on retail options trading demand rather than LP yield enhancement
- Key challenge: proving customer demand for options vs futures
- Geographic considerations: Options trading popular in US, Japan, Korea, India
- Suggested shorter expiry options (1-5 minutes) as potential opportunity
