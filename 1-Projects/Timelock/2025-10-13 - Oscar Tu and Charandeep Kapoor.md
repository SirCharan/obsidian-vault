### Timelock Product Overview

- Oscar Tu from SCB/Susquehanna Crypto (tech/investing background, worked on lending protocols)
- Timelock eliminates liquidations through Uniswap V3 liquidity borrowing mechanism

- Traders pay upfront premium for position exposure (like options)
- No peer-to-peer derivative structure - profits/losses from asset price appreciation
- ADL (auto-deleveraging) problems from yesterday’s market events demonstrate need
- Two vault types for liquidity providers:

- Uniswap V3 vaults (managed by Gyro Finance/Charm5 partners)
- Stable vaults (delta-neutral via shorting perps, managed by ST Digital)
- TUSD stablecoin offering 15-18% APR (higher than Athena’s model)
- Perpetual options capability planned (SBF’s concept nobody implemented)
