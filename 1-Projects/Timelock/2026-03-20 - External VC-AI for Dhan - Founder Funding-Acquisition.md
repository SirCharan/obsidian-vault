Charandeep has developed multiple AI-focused trading solutions independently:

- Fine-tuned Claude Heiko model for trading that doubled 15 lakh capital in under one year
- Stokie Terminal: Bloomberg-style product integrating 100+ news sources, AI insights, weather monitoring, and geopolitical tracking
- ChatGPT for stock market using multi-agent architecture

## Technical Architecture Discussion

**System Design:** Six agents distributed across three language models

- 8B model handles intent parsing
- 70B model manages news summarization
- Largest model generates deep research

**Key Features:** Debate orchestrator creates opposing viewpoints before generating conclusions

**Data Integration:** Technical APIs, fundamental data sources, Bloomberg/Twitter news scraping, satellite weather data

**Cost Optimization:** Regex and NLP filtering applied before AI processing

---

## Market Insights & User Behavior

- English language barriers create high user drop-off among Indian users
- Regional markets (Maharashtra, Gujarat) require voice-first interface design
- News aggregation identified as primary value proposition
- Users prefer summaries over complete articles
- Strong demand for sector-specific analysis and comparison tools
- Institutional interest from hedge funds, consulting firms, and reinsurance sectors (Lloyd's of London)
