### Timekeeper Background & Previous Work

- Founded Timekeeper after trading background at hedge fund (delta neutral yield strategies, arbitrage)
- Previously built DeFi products:

- Buffer Finance (binary options, got hacked, team moved to Hyperliquid)
- Metlab (liquidation-free lending/borrowing, $50M TVL on Mantle)
- Puff the Dragon meme coin (reached $100M market cap, currently $40-50M)
- Current product: Timelock - offering perpetuals and binary options without liquidations

### Current Timelock Products

- Perpetuals trading platform:

- 400x leverage on ETH with only $10 USDC risk
- Custom duration (1 hour to 7 days)
- No liquidations - max loss capped at premium paid
- Can extend trades indefinitely or close anytime
- Options platform:

- First DeFi product enabling custom strike prices and durations
- Down to 5-second precision (Monad’s minimum limitation)
- Market makers at Token expressed strong interest
- Designed for both retail and institutional use

### Major Roadblocks & Pivot Strategy

- Core problems preventing Timelock launch:

- Need audits but no funding for them
- Liquidity providers demanding 30% fixed yield (rejected even with STS Digital backing)
- VCs hesitant about Monad until mainnet goes live
- Proposed pivot to meme coin launchpad:

- Would solve liquidity problem through fees
- Different branding (considering “FOMO pun”)
- Key differentiators: day-zero perpetuals and binary options on meme coins
- Share 50% of swap fees with creators
- Direct Uniswap V3 graduation (no bonding curves)

### Pareen’s Strategic Concerns

- Power law dynamics in launchpads:

- Top player gets 60-80% market share, second gets 10-15%, third gets nothing
- Distribution and mindshare more critical than product features
- Existing launchpads have year-long head start in community building
- Risk of founder energy dispersion:

- 3-6 months minimum to compete with established players
- Would dilute focus from core Timelock products
- Success depends on marketing/social skills, not technical product
- Recommended validating with meme coin creators who launched $50M+ coins before proceeding

### Partnership & Growth Support Offers

- Pareen offered weekly 30-minute calls for distribution/marketing improvement
- Specific areas for improvement:

- Twitter strategy and authentic voice development
- Finding first 50-100 engaged followers
- Balancing meme content with technical product content
- Alternative suggested: focus on securing LPs for Timelock rather than pivot
- Mentioned potential introductions to other founders and growth experts
- Offered to check with CJ about liquidity provider issues and grant possibilities
