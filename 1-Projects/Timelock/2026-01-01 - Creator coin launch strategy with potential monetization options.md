### Creator Coin Platform Concept

- Building comprehensive platform on existing audited infrastructure

- Perps, options, and launchpad already developed
- Token creation with Uniswap V3 liquidity deployment
- Integration with social media for creator verification
- Creator monetization through multiple revenue streams

- Direct token revenue sharing
- Long-term locked token benefits
- Protection against pump-and-dump schemes

### Chain Selection & Technical Implementation

- Hyperliquid preferred over Base or Monad

- Larger scope potential
- Better integration possibilities
- Technical components:

1. Token launchpad
2. Perpetuals and options trading
3. Prediction markets
4. Liquid staking derivatives (T-HYPE token concept)
- Revenue distribution: 50% to creators, time-locked tokens generate additional yield

### Creator Acquisition Strategy

- Target creators with 1K+ followers initially

- No gatekeeping based on follower count
- Focus on authentic creators vs. bot accounts
- Need 5-10 committed creators to launch
- Platform prevents manipulation through short-selling options
- Anti-scam measures built into tokenomics

### Compensation Structure

- Founding team member role offer

- ₹100K annual salary
- 0.1% equity with 4-year vesting
- Product development and creator outreach responsibilities
- Points system for early adopters

- First 5 creators get premium point allocation
- Points convert to token airdrop at TGE
- Higher rewards for early marketing and user education

### Next Steps

- Decision needed on chain selection (Hyperliquid preferred)
- Identify 5-10 interested creators to begin with
- Finalize role structure and compensation details
- Begin technical development once creator interest confirmed
