### TimeLog Product & Innovation

- Oracle-less leverage trading using Uniswap v3 LP liquidity
- Key innovation: Borrowing tick liquidity from Uniswap v3 LPs outside the pool

- Traders pay premium (similar to options) for leveraged exposure
- No liquidations, maximum loss is premium paid
- Example: $6 premium for 1 ETH upside exposure over 1 hour
- Pricing advantage: 0.7x to 0.9x Black-Scholes vs 1.0x on competitors
- Live demo on Base testnet fork (demo.timelog.trade)
- Expanding to custom durations for event trading (Fed announcements, earnings)
