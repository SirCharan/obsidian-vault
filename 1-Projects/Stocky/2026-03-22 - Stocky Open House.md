# Stocky Open House - Meeting Notes

**Date:** Sunday, March 22, 2026
**Creator:** Charandeep Kapoor
**Attendees:** 13 participants including Pranabdutta004, Visshal36, Rahulballiabi, Suryasudhakar1999, Antony Pulicken, and others

## Product Performance Highlights

Stocky's AI trading system achieved "100% returns (16L from 15L capital) in first year" with 51 consecutive winning days initially. The system demonstrated a 73% win rate across 48 commodity trades, with average gains of Rs.46,000 versus losses of Rs.27,000. The Sharpe ratio reached 2.29, exceeding most Indian hedge funds.

## Global Expansion & Notable Trades

The platform expanded beyond Indian markets into Polymarket (50% gains in 4 months), crypto, forex, weather, and event-based markets. Two significant examples were highlighted:

**Iran Strait of Hormuz:** Detected shipping closure via satellite/AIS data at 7:15 UTC before media coverage at 4 PM. Executed seven coordinated trades across crude oil, gold, indices, and bitcoin.

**Weather Prediction:** Identified Pacific temperature patterns and predicted monsoon impact, resulting in a 27% gain on National Fertilizers Limited (Rs.120 to Rs.150).

## Product Portfolio

**Stocky Terminal** aggregates Bloomberg news and integrates satellite data for global event tracking.

**Stocky LLM** employs six specialized agents (technical, fundamental, news, macro, risk analysis) with a debate orchestrator assigning weights and confidence scoring (80+ good, 90+ excellent).

**Trading Interface** provides 5-minute crypto signals with 82% confidence, with planned mobile-first design and broker integration.

## Technical Infrastructure

The system uses fine-tuned Claude Haiku via MCP server connection to Zerodha, undisclosed satellite sources, Twitter scraping, and live market data APIs facing rate-limiting challenges.

## Business Model

Currently free during development, with planned Rs.1,000/month LLM subscription and Rs.20/order sub-broker model. Monthly infrastructure costs: $1,200-1,300. Free access extends approximately three months maximum.

## Fundraising

Target: $1-1.5M at $10M valuation. Interest from Zerodha (Nitin Kamath), Accel, Futures First, and Dubai hedge funds. Alternative revenue strategies include data/insights sales to institutional investors.

## Upcoming Features

Trade execution buttons, user authentication with point systems, options dashboards (Define Edge integration), support/resistance indicators, mobile scalping app, and broker integrations starting with Zerodha.

## Regulatory Framework

SEBI constraints prohibit profit-sharing and direct client trading, but permit AI insights and self-trading APIs. Sub-broker model remains compliant for order execution.

## Next Steps

Weekly feedback sessions, WhatsApp support group, early-user point allocation, authentication rollout, and Tuesday options dashboard delivery.
