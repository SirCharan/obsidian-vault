# Meeting Notes: "With Chanchal sir"

**Date:** March 18, 2026
**Creator:** Charandeep Kapoor

## Trading Terminal Product Strategy

- Focus on commodity trading over equity markets
- Better success rate: 50% versus 10% in stock market
- 24-hour trading window offers flexibility
- Target demographic: 18-30 year-olds (similar to Dream11 users)
- Gold trading appeals strongly to retail investors

**Key insight:** "The easier the app, the more money you lose" -- Zerodha's simplicity correlates with higher user losses, while complex platforms like ICICI Direct produce better outcomes. Balance usability with protective friction.

## Market Opportunity & User Behavior

- New account signups rising significantly
- Trading shifting from gambling mentality to business/profit opportunity
- User pain point: Decision-making takes 10+ minutes analyzing charts
- Goal: Reduce decision time to 10 seconds using AI

**Technical automation potential:** EMA9/EMA21 crossovers, RSI, MACD indicators with real-time notifications

## Product Development Approach

- Build Progressive Web App (PWA) first
- Delay full product reveal until securing broker license
- Marketing: Rs.100 Instagram ads targeting 50 initial signups
- Waitlist strategy: Rs.10 per monthly user
- Demo-first approach: video demonstration instead of pitch deck
