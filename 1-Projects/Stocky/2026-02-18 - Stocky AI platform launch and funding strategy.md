### Stocky Product Demo & Pitch

- AI-powered trading platform combining Zerodha, MoneyControl, and TradingView functionality
- Built on fine-tuned Claude 3.5 Sonnet model with custom MCP server
- Key features:

- Plain English queries for market analysis
- Real-time news aggregation and summarization
- Technical/fundamental analysis with 1-10 scoring system
- One-click trade execution through Zerodha integration
- Portfolio management and position tracking

- Personal trading results over 8 months:

- Initial capital: ₹15 lakhs

- Minimal drawdowns achieved

- Started as Telegram bot
- 250+ users in testing
