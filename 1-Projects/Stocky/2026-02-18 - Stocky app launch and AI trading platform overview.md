### Stocky Product Vision

- AI-powered trading platform combining multiple Indian stock market tools

- Integrates Zerodha, Groww, Mint, MoneyControl functionality
- Single platform approach vs. fragmented tool ecosystem
- Natural language trading interface

- Users can execute trades through simple English commands
- Eliminates need for complex trading interfaces

### Technical Foundation & Performance

- Built on Claude 3.5 Sonnet with custom MCP server

- Fine-tuned specifically for Indian stock market trading
- 6+ months development timeline since Claude MCP launch
- Live trading results with ₹1,314 lakhs capital

- Generated 120% returns over 8 months
- Real money validation of AI trading capabilities

### Market Validation & User Testing

- 250+ users tested via Telegram bot over several months

- Significant user demand to expand beyond closed testing
- Positive response drove decision to build full platform
- Now live with proprietary charting and LLM integration

- Custom chart interface developed
- Moved beyond bot-based interaction model

### Business Development

- High user demand for public service launch

- Multiple requests to open platform to broader market
- Transition from internal tool to commercial product
- Platform now ready for wider market deployment

- Technical infrastructure proven
- User validation completed
