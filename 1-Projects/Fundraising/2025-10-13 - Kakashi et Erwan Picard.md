### IEXEC Overview & Positioning

- EVM foundation providing privacy solutions to DeFi ecosystems
- Founded 2017, raised 10,000 BTC in ICO - well-funded company
- Uses trusted execution environments (TEEs) with Intel SGX/TDX
- Recently launched on Arbitrum (September 2024) after own Bellco sidechain

- Targeting DeFi attention on major chain vs. gas-free sidechain

### Privacy Messaging Solution

- Core offering: encrypted user communications without doxxing
- User flow:

- Connect wallet → encrypt email/Telegram handle
- IEXEC generates separate EVM address as symmetric key
- TimeVault sends messages via address without knowing actual handles
- Cost: ~4 cents for 5,000 messages (Arbitrum gas fees only)
- Users pay single 3-cent signature for data encryption, then free

### TimeVault Integration Use Cases

- Notification system for trading activities:

- Margin calls before trade closure
- Options exercise alerts
- Trade completion confirmations
- Two notification types:

- Personal: user-specific trading updates
- Broadcast: new features/listings to all users
- Enhanced UX: post-trade popup offering notification signup vs. generic signup page

### Grant Structure & Metrics

- Fixed grant: $6-7K range (between $4-10K based on traction)
- Variable component: additional funding based on encrypted user adoption

- Tiers at 1,000, 5,000, 10,000+ encrypted users
- IEXEC needs TimeVault metrics: daily/monthly users, partnerships, ecosystem data
- White-label option available (no IEXEC branding required)
