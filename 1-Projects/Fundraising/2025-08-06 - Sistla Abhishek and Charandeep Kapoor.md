### StreamSwap Overview

- Time-based token protocol for raising capital over streaming periods

- Example: Lab token locked 75k tokens, raised 2.4M OSMO over 1 hour
- Final price: ~$42 per lab token (10 tokens = 1 NFT at $420)
- Anti-sniping mechanism: attackers need proportionally larger capital

- 24-hour stream requires 24x capital to attack with same amount
- Favors serious participants over small-scale snipers
- Fair launch model similar to crowdfunding + auction hybrid

- No instant swaps like Uniswap - tokens distributed over time
- Two phases: boot shopping (deposits only) + streaming (actual swaps)
