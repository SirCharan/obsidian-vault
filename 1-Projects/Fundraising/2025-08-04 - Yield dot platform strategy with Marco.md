### Yield Platform Strategy Overview

- Simple platform for investment managers offering low-risk delta neutral returns
- Target: ~15% yields in crypto markets for LPs
- Accept USDT, USDC and other stablecoins across multiple chains
- Strategy: Deploy into existing DeFi vaults rather than building native strategies

- Pass through all vault costs to users
- No fees charged for core service

### Initial Implementation Plan

- Manual bridging to Hyperliquid initially
- Manual deposits into Hyperbeat vault
- Frontend requirements:

- Deposit/withdrawal functionality for stablecoins
- Historical returns graph
- User returns dashboard showing fees, costs, breakdown
- Target TVL: $1-2M within one week of launch

### Revenue Model

- No direct fees on core service
- Revenue from points and airdrops only
- Example: 15% yield on $1M = $150k total

- Traditional 10-20% cut would be $15-30k annually
- Instead focusing on airdrop/point farming income

### Future Expansion Plans

- Develop proprietary stablecoin
- List on Morpho, Aave platforms
- Additional revenue streams from stablecoin:

- Swap fees from AMM deployment
- Lending fees from borrowing platforms
- Peg stabilization and buffer revenue

### Next Steps

- Build basic frontend (suggested using Lovable)
- Get design feedback from Kesha or other contacts
- Present to first investor after design finalization
- Research alternative yield sources beyond Hyperbeat
